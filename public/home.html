What is BuilderAudit?

BuilderAudit is a free AI-powered website audit for UK building companies. Paste any builder's URL and, in about 60 seconds, it returns a trust score out of 100 across photo authenticity, trust signals, mobile experience, site speed and quote flow — plus a Companies House check. Builders use it to fix their site; homeowners use it to vet a builder before hiring.

The 9-point check

We look at the same stuff a homeowner does — just faster

Nine things that decide whether someone trusts you enough to ask for a quote, or scrolls on.

Site speed
How fast pages load on a homeowner's phone.
Mobile friendly
Does it actually work on a 6-inch screen?
Photo quality
Sharp, real photos of finished work — or grainy stock.
Trust signals
Reviews, FMB / TrustMark, public liability insurance.
Services clarity
Can a homeowner tell what you actually do?
Quote flow
How easy is it to actually ask you for a price?
SEO & Google
Will anyone find you when they're searching?
Copy & tone
Does it sound like a builder or a robot?
Real vs AI photos
We flag stock and AI-generated job photos.
